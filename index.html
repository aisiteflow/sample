The Aura Narrative

Crafting Elegance

We believe objects carry memory. Our studio began in 2022 as a collaborative experiment between architects, botanists, and cosmetic scientists to capture weights, shadows, and natural scents in physical formulations.

Every jar is blended and individually inspected. Our clean formulas are rooted in botanical science, ensuring your space remains free of synthetics.

Our Journey

The Evolution of Calm

2022

Born from Silence

Aura Shops was established in a quiet forest cabin, combining architectural diffusers with high-grade organic oils to restore sensory balance.

2024

The Toxin-Free Pledge

We successfully eliminated all paraffin wax from our candles and artificial chemicals from our mist lines, moving exclusively to local botanical growers.

2026

Global Sanctuary Curation

Partnerships with world-class wellness spaces to curate ambient aesthetics. Recognized for our signature anti-gravity Soft UI architecture.
